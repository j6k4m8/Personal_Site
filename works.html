Dash, built at HopHacks in Spring 2015, is an app that will revolutionize fitness. Dash provides the opportunity to incorporate a friendly wager into running goals. Users can schedule their races within the Dash interface, which allows them to set an endpoint and the amount each competitor must wager to enter the race. It's simple: first person to reach the end marker wins the prize. Dash is an Android app (built in Android Studio) with a Node.js backend which incorporates the Google Maps and Venmo APIs.
